Project Overview

Urban–Rural Lifestyle
Differences in Bhutan

I am Sonam Peldon, Gedu College of Business Studies intern and data practitioner at Data Scientists Pvt. Ltd., exploring how lifestyle habits affect health and wellbeing. I created this project to examine how daily routines such as sleep, work, and screen use differ between urban and rural residents in Bhutan and how these differences influence sleep quality and daytime energy. Using these insights, I aim to develop evidence-based tools, like a website and mobile app, to help individuals and communities improve their daily habits and overall wellbeing.
